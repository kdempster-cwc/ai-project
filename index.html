About Me

As the Instructional Technology Specialist at Central Wyoming College, I am dedicated to bridging the gap between pedagogy and technology. Situated within the Teaching & Learning Center, my mission is to empower faculty and staff with the digital tools necessary to create engaging, accessible, and effective learning environments.

I provide leadership and support for the college's learning management systems and educational technologies, ensuring that CWC remains at the forefront of digital education trends in the Wyoming community college system.

Department Mission

The Department of Teaching & Learning at CWC supports academic excellence by fostering a culture of continuous improvement. We collaborate with faculty to design innovative curriculum, integrate technology seamlessly into the classroom, and support the delivery of high-quality distance education.
